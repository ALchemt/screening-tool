# AI Automation Engineer @ FlowHR

## About FlowHR

FlowHR builds AI-powered automation tools for mid-market HR teams, eliminating repetitive tasks in onboarding, offboarding, and compliance workflows. We serve 200+ customers across North America and are backed by Craft Ventures and Operator Partners.

## Role

We're looking for a junior AI Automation Engineer to design and deploy no-code/low-code workflows that connect our customers' HR systems (BambooHR, Rippling, Workday) with LLM APIs to automate document generation, candidate screening, and policy Q&A. You'll work directly with our customer success team to understand pain points, prototype solutions in n8n, and iterate based on feedback. This is a high-impact role where your work will directly reduce manual workload for HR teams.

## Responsibilities

- Build and maintain n8n workflows integrating OpenAI/Anthropic APIs with HR SaaS platforms via REST and webhooks
- Create automated pipelines for resume parsing, offer letter generation, and policy document summarization
- Write and refine prompts for GPT-4 and Claude 3.5 to ensure high-quality output in HR contexts
- Monitor workflow performance, debug failures, and implement error handling and retry logic
- Document workflows and create internal playbooks for common automation patterns
- Collaborate with customer success to scope new automation requests and estimate effort
- Participate in weekly demos showcasing new automations to internal stakeholders

## Required Qualifications

- 1-2 years experience building automation workflows (n8n, Zapier, Make, or similar)
- Hands-on experience calling OpenAI or Anthropic APIs (GPT-4, Claude 3+)
- Proficiency with REST APIs, JSON, and webhook concepts
- Basic understanding of OAuth 2.0 and API authentication patterns
- Ability to write clear technical documentation for non-technical users
- Comfortable working in a fast-paced startup environment with shifting priorities
- Strong communication skills and customer empathy

## Nice-to-Have

- Experience with HR tech platforms (BambooHR, Greenhouse, Lever, Workday)
- Familiarity with Python or JavaScript for custom scripting
- Understanding of GDPR and HR data privacy requirements
- Prior experience in a customer-facing technical role

## What We Offer

- $2,000/year learning and conference budget
- Health, dental, vision (100% covered for employee)
