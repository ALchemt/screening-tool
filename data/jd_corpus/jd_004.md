# Voice AI Engineer @ HealthBridge AI

## About HealthBridge AI

HealthBridge AI automates patient intake, appointment scheduling, and prescription refill calls for medical practices. We've handled 200k+ voice interactions for 50+ clinics, reducing front-desk workload by 60%.

## Role

We're seeking a Voice AI Engineer to design and deploy conversational agents that replace traditional IVR systems for healthcare workflows. You'll integrate Twilio Voice with LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic) and voice AI platforms (Vapi, Retell AI, or Bland AI) to build agents that handle appointment scheduling, insurance verification, and prescription refills. You'll own the full stack from telephony setup to conversation design, testing, and production deployment.

## Responsibilities

- Architect and deploy voice agents using Twilio Programmable Voice + OpenAI Realtime API or Vapi/Retell
- Design conversation flows with multi-turn dialogue, interruption handling, and context retention
- Integrate voice agents with practice management systems (Athenahealth, Epic, SimplePractice) via REST APIs
- Implement call routing logic, transfer protocols, and fallback to human agents
- Build real-time transcription and sentiment analysis pipelines for call quality monitoring
- Optimize for low latency (<2s response time) and natural conversation pacing
- Conduct A/B tests on prompt variations and measure conversation success rates (completion, accuracy)

- 3+ years experience building voice or conversational AI applications
- Deep expertise with Twilio Voice API (TwiML, webhooks, call control)
- Hands-on experience integrating LLMs (GPT-4, Claude 3.5) into real-time conversational systems
- Proficiency with at least one voice AI platform (Vapi, Retell AI, Bland AI, or similar)

- Comfortable with HIPAA compliance requirements and PHI handling
