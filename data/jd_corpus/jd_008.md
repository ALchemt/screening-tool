# AI Solutions Engineer @ Forge DevTools

## About Forge DevTools

Forge DevTools provides AI-powered code review, test generation, and documentation tools for engineering teams at high-growth startups. Our platform integrates with GitHub, GitLab, and Bitbucket and is used by 300+ engineering teams.

## Role

We're seeking an AI Solutions Engineer to work directly with customers to scope, design, and implement custom AI automation projects. You'll split your time between hands-on engineering (building proofs-of-concept, integrating our APIs into customer workflows) and consulting (requirements gathering, technical workshops, success planning). This is a hybrid IC + customer-facing role for someone who loves solving complex problems and explaining technical concepts to both engineers and non-technical stakeholders.

## Responsibilities

- Lead technical discovery calls with prospects and customers to understand their automation needs and pain points
- Design and build custom AI automation solutions using our platform APIs, n8n, and LLM integrations
- Develop proofs-of-concept and demos that showcase AI capabilities tailored to customer use cases
- Provide technical guidance on prompt engineering, RAG architecture, and agent design patterns
- Collaborate with sales and customer success to scope projects, estimate effort, and define success metrics
- Deliver technical workshops and training sessions for customer engineering teams
- Gather product feedback and work with engineering to prioritize feature requests based on customer needs

## Required Qualifications

- 3+ years in a solutions engineering, sales engineering, or technical consulting role
- Strong hands-on engineering skills with Python, REST APIs, and cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Deep experience with OpenAI, Anthropic, or other LLM APIs in production environments
- Proven ability to scope and deliver technical projects with ambiguous requirements
- Excellent communication skills and comfort presenting to both technical and executive audiences
- Experience with developer tools, CI/CD, or DevOps workflows
- Willingness to travel occasionally for customer meetings and conferences (10-15%)

## Nice-to-Have

- Experience with code analysis tools (SonarQube, CodeClimate, Snyk)
- Familiarity with AI code generation tools (GitHub Copilot, Cursor, Cody)
- Background in pre-sales or post-sales technical roles at a B2B SaaS company
- Knowledge of LangChain, LlamaIndex, or agent frameworks
- Experience building on GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket APIs
